Abstract

The sudden growth of cryptocurrencies has led to a highly volatile and dynamic financial market. This project aims to categorize cryptocurrencies into Large-cap, Mid-cap, and Small-cap categories to better understand market trends.

By leveraging market value, trading volume, and historical prices from reliable APIs, we investigate how market capitalization relies on price volatility and liquidity. Our findings reveal that big-cap cryptos offer stability, while smaller-cap coins present higher volatility and risk.
